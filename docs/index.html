REAL WORLD TECHNOLOGIES

Create connections, decompose complex problems into simpler parts, allow students to innovate, and enhance their understanding of real-world technology with the Explore IoT Kit - an industry-standard IoT tool that will help prepare them for their future careers.

STEP BY STEP ACTIVITIES

Students learn how to build internet-connected objects by following step-by- step activities, which teach them to write, deploy, and interact with code through a web based integrated development environment (IDE) and cloud- based dashboards.

LEARNING BY DOING APPROACH

All the activities adopt a learning-by-doing approach, through which students acquire knowledge by constructing fully functional solutions. The activities are linked to everyday life, providing students with an understanding of advanced technology.
